The Store-house of God: Helping the Believer in Time of Need

"Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this," says the LORD Almighty, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it." — Malachi 3:10

The Store-house of God is a divinely inspired platform established on the biblical principle of communal stewardship and mutual care among believers. Just as the Israelites were commanded to bring their tithes and offerings into the storehouse so that there would be provision for all, this platform serves as a modern-day storehouse where believers come together to deposit their resources, bless one another, and ensure that no member of the body of Christ goes without.

The Store-house operates on the understanding that all wealth belongs to God and that we are merely stewards of what He has entrusted to us. When we give freely and cheerfully, we activate the principles of sowing and reaping, and God multiplies our seed. The Fisherman's Coin system is designed to facilitate the exchange of blessings among believers, creating a cycle of generosity that glorifies God.

The purpose of this Store-house is threefold: (1) To provide a structured and transparent platform for believers to manage their spiritual and financial commitments; (2) To foster a community of generosity where believers support one another in times of need; and (3) To demonstrate the love of Christ through practical action, showing that the Kingdom of God is alive and active in our daily lives.

As it is written: "Suppose a brother or a sister is without clothes and daily food. If one of you says to them, 'Go in peace; keep warm and well fed,' but does nothing about their physical needs, what good is it?" — James 2:15-16. The Store-house ensures that we do not merely speak words of blessing but act upon them.

⚖ The Seven Pillars of the Store-house ⚖

"Wisdom has built her house; she has set up its seven pillars." — Proverbs 9:1

I

The Pillar of FAITHFUL STEWARDSHIP

"Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much, and whoever is dishonest with very little will also be dishonest with much." — Luke 16:10

The first and foremost pillar upon which the Store-house stands is Faithful Stewardship. Every coin deposited, every offering sent, and every withdrawal made is managed with the utmost integrity and transparency. We recognize that we are not owners but stewards of God's resources. The Store-house pledges to manage all funds with accountability, regular audits, and open reporting to all members.

Just as Joseph was faithful in Potiphar's house and later in all of Egypt, so too must the managers of this Store-house be found faithful. Every transaction is recorded, every coin is tracked, and every member can verify the movement of resources. We hold to the principle that "it is required that those who have been given a trust must prove faithful" — 1 Corinthians 4:2.

II

The Pillar of COMMUNAL GENEROSITY

"All the believers were together and had everything in common. They sold property and possessions to give to anyone who had need." — Acts 2:44-45

The second pillar is Communal Generosity, rooted in the early church's practice of sharing all things in common. The Store-house is not a savings account for individual enrichment — it is a living, breathing community where resources flow freely among believers according to need and generosity.

The offering system allows believers to send blessings directly to fellow members. The 45% pool distribution ensures that the community as a whole benefits from the collective deposits. This pillar reminds us that "none of the members of the church shall be in need" when we practice radical generosity as commanded in Acts and modeled by the early disciples.

III

The Pillar of SACRED TITHING

"'Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this,' says the LORD Almighty, 'and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven.'" — Malachi 3:10

The third pillar is Sacred Tithing. The Store-house is built upon the ancient and unchanging principle that the first portion of our increase belongs to the LORD. When believers deposit funds and purchase Fisherman's Coins, they are engaging in a modern expression of the tithe — setting apart the first fruits of their labor for God's purposes.

This pillar teaches us that tithing is not a burden but a blessing. It is the one area where God invites us to test Him. When we are faithful in bringing our tithes, God promises to open the windows of heaven. The Store-house facilitates this sacred act, ensuring that every believer has the opportunity to participate in the covenant promise of provision.

IV

The Pillar of DISCIPLE MULTIPLICATION

"Then Jesus came to them and said, 'All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Therefore go and make disciples of all nations.'" — Matthew 28:18-19

The fourth pillar is Disciple Multiplication. The referral system is not merely a marketing tool — it is an expression of the Great Commission. When believers introduce others to the Store-house, they are fulfilling the command to make disciples. Every referral represents a soul drawn closer to the community of faith.

The rewards distributed through the referral system recognize and honor those who actively participate in growing the Kingdom. As Andrew brought his brother Peter to Jesus, and Philip brought Nathanael, so too are we called to bring others into the fellowship. The 45% pool distribution incentivizes and rewards this Kingdom work.

V

The Pillar of TRANSPARENT JUSTICE

"The integrity of the upright guides them, but the unfaithful are destroyed by their duplicity." — Proverbs 11:3

The fifth pillar is Transparent Justice. Every transaction, every deposit, every withdrawal, and every distribution is recorded and visible. The Store-house operates on the principle that there should be no hidden dealings among God's people. As Paul wrote: "For we are taking pains to do what is right, not only in the eyes of the Lord but also in the eyes of man" — 2 Corinthians 8:21.

This pillar ensures that all members have access to their transaction history, can verify pool distributions, and trust that the system operates fairly. The withdrawal policy, the distribution formula, and the referral rewards are all governed by clear, unchanging rules that apply equally to every member regardless of status.

VI

The Pillar of URGENT COMPASSION

"Suppose a brother or a sister is without clothes and daily food. If one of you says to them, 'Go in peace; keep warm and well fed,' but does nothing about their physical needs, what good is it?" — James 2:15-16

The sixth pillar is Urgent Compassion. The Store-house exists not as a financial institution but as a ministry of mercy. Its primary purpose is to help believers in their time of need — whether that need is physical, emotional, or spiritual. The withdrawal system is designed to provide timely assistance to those who face hardship.

This pillar is embodied in the words of Jesus: "Whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me" — Matthew 25:40. When a believer makes a withdrawal from the Store-house, they are not taking from others but receiving from the bounty that God has provided through the faithful giving of the community. The Store-house stands as a testament that God provides through His people.

VII

The Pillar of ENDURING TRUST

"And my God will meet all your needs according to the riches of his glory in Christ Jesus." — Philippians 4:19

The seventh and final pillar is Enduring Trust. The Store-house is ultimately built on the unwavering trust that God is our Provider — Jehovah Jireh. Every mechanism, every rule, and every process in this platform points back to the truth that God supplies all our needs according to His riches in glory.

This pillar reminds us that the Store-house is not a bank, not an investment scheme, not a financial product — it is a living expression of faith. When we deposit, we trust God. When we give offerings, we trust God. When we wait for distribution, we trust God. The entire system is an exercise in faith, and the reward is not just financial but eternal. As the Psalmist declares: "Trust in the LORD with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight" — Proverbs 3:5-6.

Terms of Use & Procedures

1. Account Registration

Every believer must register with accurate personal information. One account per person is permitted. Multiple accounts will result in permanent suspension. By registering, you agree to the principles of the Store-house and commit to operating in integrity.

2. Deposits & EFT Transfers

All deposits must be made via Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) to the designated Store-house bank account. Proof of payment must be submitted through the platform. Deposits are verified and credited within 1-24 hours. The minimum deposit is R10.00.

3. Fisherman's Coins

1 Fisherman's Coin = R50.00 (South African Rand). Coins are purchased using your deposited wallet balance. Coins cannot be directly refunded to cash but can be used for offerings or transferred to other believers. The coin value remains fixed.

4. Offerings

Believers may send coins as offerings to any registered member. Offerings are a gesture of blessing and fellowship and are non-reversible once sent. The offering system is designed to facilitate generosity, not commerce. All offerings are final.

5. Pool Distribution

45% of the total pool (accumulated from deposits and offerings) is distributed among eligible members. Distribution criteria include: active account status, participation in offerings, and referral activity. Distributions are calculated and processed on a monthly cycle.

6. Withdrawals

Members may withdraw from their deposited wallet balance at any time. Minimum withdrawal: R50.00. A 5% administration fee applies. Withdrawals are processed within 24-48 hours via EFT to the member's registered bank account. Withdrawals do not affect purchased coins.

7. Referral Rewards

Members earn rewards when referred users actively participate in the platform. Referral rewards are drawn from the 45% pool distribution. Manipulation of the referral system through fake accounts or fraudulent activity will result in permanent ban.

8. Community Conduct

The Temple Chat is a space for edifying fellowship. Harassment, hate speech, solicitation, or any form of abuse will not be tolerated. All members are expected to conduct themselves with the love, grace, and respect befitting the body of Christ.

9. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes will be resolved according to Matthew 18:15-17: first in private, then with witnesses, then before the community leadership. The Store-house leadership team has final authority in all matters of dispute.

10. Limitation of Liability

The Store-house operates in good faith. However, all financial activities carry inherent risk. Members are encouraged to give only what they can afford and to seek God's wisdom in all financial decisions. The Store-house is not a bank and deposits are not covered by deposit insurance.

The Store-house Pool is the heart of our community. It is a shared fund that grows from the faithful giving of every believer and returns blessings equally to all active members. Here is how it works, step by step:

💰 1. How the Pool Grows

Every time a believer makes a deposit or sends an offering, the Rand value flows into the Store-house Pool:

  • EFT Deposits — When you deposit funds via EFT to the Store-house bank account, the full amount goes into your wallet balance and contributes to the pool.
  • Offerings — When you send Fisherman's Coins as an offering to another believer, the Rand value of those coins (R50 per coin) is added to the pool.
  • Coin Purchases — When you buy Fisherman's Coins with your deposited balance, the coin value contributes to the pool.

Example: If three believers deposit R500 each and send R100 in offerings, the pool grows by R1,800.

📈 2. The 45% Distribution

Every 7 days, 45% of the total pool is automatically distributed equally among all active believers. This is not optional — it is built into the system and happens automatically, like clockwork.

  • Automatic — No admin action needed. The system distributes every 7 days.
  • Equal split — Every active believer receives the same amount, regardless of how much they deposited. The blessing is shared equally, just as the early church shared all things in common (Acts 2:44-45).
  • Credited to wallet — The distribution goes directly into your wallet balance, where you can use it to buy coins, send offerings, or withdraw.

Example: Pool = R1,800. 45% = R810. 3 active believers = R270 each credited to their wallets.

📅 3. The 7-Day Cycle

The distribution runs on a weekly cycle:

  1. Day 1 — A distribution occurs. All active believers receive their equal share.
  2. Days 2-7 — The pool continues to grow as believers deposit funds and send offerings.
  3. Day 7 — The next distribution occurs with the new, larger pool.

This creates a cycle of growth: as more people participate, the pool grows, and each weekly distribution becomes larger. The dashboard shows a live countdown to your next distribution.

☞ 4. Who Qualifies?

To receive pool distributions, you must be:

  • Active — Your account must be active (not suspended or deactivated).
  • A Believer — Only members with the "Believer" role qualify. Admin accounts do not receive distributions.
  • Registered — You must have a registered account on the platform.

The more believers who join and participate, the larger the pool grows, and the more everyone receives.

💰 5. Where Does the Other 55% Go?

The remaining 55% of the pool stays in the Store-house to sustain operations, cover withdrawal processing fees, maintain the platform, and ensure the long-term health of the community. This is consistent with the principle of stewardship: "Whoever can be trusted with very little can also be trusted with much" — Luke 16:10.

🏆 6. Summary

  • Pool source: Deposits + Offerings + Coin purchases
  • Distribution: 45% of total pool, every 7 days, automatically
  • Split: Equal among all active believers
  • Where it goes: Your wallet balance (can withdraw, buy coins, or send offerings)
  • Remaining 55%: Retained for operations and platform sustainability
